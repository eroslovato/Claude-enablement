Try it yourself

Hands-on mini-exercises. The goal is to have at least one successful interaction before the session.

Exercise 1: Your first conversation
Open Claude Chat. Pick a customer account you know well. Ask: "I have a meeting with [company] tomorrow. Based on what you know, help me prepare talking points about their process mining maturity." See what happens.
Exercise
Exercise 2: Create an artifact
Ask Claude to create something visual: "Create a one-page executive summary of Celonis Process Intelligence for a CFO audience." Claude will produce an artifact you can preview, edit, and download.
Exercise
Exercise 3: Try Cowork
Open Cowork mode in Claude Desktop. Point it at a folder with a real document (e.g., a proposal, meeting notes). Ask: "Read this document and create a structured summary with key decisions and open items." Watch it work with your files directly.
Exercise
Exercise 4: Install a skill
Browse our Celonis skills registry. Find one that looks useful for your work. Install it and try it on a real task.

Your role during the session: You're not just a participant — you're a force multiplier. During hands-on exercises, help the people at your table. Answer questions. Show your workflow. The best enablement happens peer-to-peer, not top-down.
